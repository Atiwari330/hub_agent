# True North Recovery — VIP Customer Profile

## Status
- **VIP Customer** — requires elevated attention and proactive communication
- **Dedicated Account Manager**: The Head of Client Success (Sagar) manages this account directly

## Active Engagements
- **Custom Engineering SOW (Statement of Work)**: True North has an active Statement of Work for custom engineering work. Feature requests, change requests, or system modification asks from this customer may already be scoped under this SOW.

## Handling Instructions

1. **Feature/change requests**: When True North requests a feature, system change, or customization, flag that it may be covered by their active SOW. The NEXT_ACTION must include notifying the Head of Client Success so he can cross-reference the SOW scope before his next meeting with the customer. Do NOT treat these as standard feature requests that go into a generic backlog — they require SOW-aware triage.

2. **Escalation path**: Any significant issue from True North should be routed through the Head of Client Success as the dedicated account manager. He should be aware of all open escalations, even routine ones, because he maintains the direct customer relationship and needs full visibility going into his regular meetings with them.

3. **Communication standard**: This is a high-touch customer. Response time expectations are tighter than standard — treat any gap of 24+ hours without a substantive response as high urgency. The customer should never feel like they are in a queue.

4. **Engineering coordination**: If a True North ticket involves engineering work, check whether it relates to the active SOW. If it does, the Head of Client Success needs to know so he can coordinate with engineering on SOW timelines vs. standard support timelines.
