# Claims, Billing & Rules (RCM)

## Overview

The claim lifecycle in Opus RCM / Imagine — from scrubbing through submission, acceptance/rejection/denial, and resolution. Also covers the Rule Builder for automated claim processing logic.

---

## Claim Lifecycle

### Scrubbing
- Pre-submission validation of claim data.

### FiCare Submission
- Claim submission through the FiCare clearinghouse.

### Acceptance
- Claim accepted by the payer for processing.

### Rejection vs. Denial
- **Rejection**: Claim never made it to the payer (clearinghouse or scrubbing error). Can be corrected and resubmitted.
- **Denial**: Payer received and processed the claim but declined to pay. Requires appeal or corrected claim.

## Common Issues in Support Tickets

### "My claim isn't going out"
- Check pre-submission errors, scrubbing results, and claim status.

### "I got a denial — what do I do?"
- Review the denial reason code, determine if appeal or corrected claim is appropriate.

### "What's the difference between a rejection and a denial?"
- Rejection = never reached payer. Denial = payer processed and declined.
