## Common Issues in Support Tickets

### "I don't know how to escalate this"
- Determine if the issue is Tier 1 (self-resolvable) or requires escalation. Follow the ticketing workflow.

### "Something changed and I don't know what"
- Check the audit history for recent changes to the affected record or configuration.
