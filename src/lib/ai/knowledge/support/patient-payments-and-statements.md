# Patient Payments & Statements

## Overview

Patient-facing payment and statement functionality in Opus RCM — Imagine Pay (patient portal payments), paper statements, Imagine Everywhere (automated text/email statements), statement holds, and the statement-to-collections cadence.

---

## Imagine Pay

- Patient portal for making payments.
- Credit card on file functionality.

---

## Paper Statements

- Generation and mailing of paper statements to patients.

---

## Imagine Everywhere (Automated Statements)

- Automated text and email statement delivery to patients.

---

## Statement Holds

- Holding statements at the account level to prevent delivery.

---

## Statement → Collections Cadence

- How the statement cycle progresses and when accounts move to collections.

---

## Common Issues in Support Tickets

### "Patient says they didn't receive a statement"
- Check statement delivery method (paper, email, text), verify contact info, check for statement holds.

### "Patient payment isn't reflected on the account"
- Check Imagine Pay transaction status, verify payment was processed and posted.
